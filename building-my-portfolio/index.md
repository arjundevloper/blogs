# Building My Portfolio: From Blank Canvas to Online Presence

Starting a portfolio felt overwhelming. Where do you even begin?

I started with a blank page, a rough sketch, and way too many browser tabs open. The goal was simple — a clean space to showcase my work without the noise.

I picked a minimal stack, focused on fast load times, and made peace with the fact that **done beats perfect**. Fonts took longer to choose than the entire layout. Classic.

Three days, two redesigns, and one accidental deployment later — it was live.

The biggest lesson? Build it for yourself first. The right people will notice.
